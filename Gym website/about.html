Common Exercises

The following exercises are encouraged for our clients as part of their daily fitness regimen.

Burpee
Engage in the dynamic full-body exercise of burpees to boost strength and endurance. Start from a standing position, transition into a squat, extend your hands forward, kick your feet back and forward rapidly, and conclude with a powerful jump from a squatted stance. View Example.
Reference
Push-Ups
Assume plank position, hands shoulder-width apart. Lower body until chest nears the ground, then push back up, keeping a straight line from head to heels. View Example.
Reference
Pull-Up
Hang from a bar with palms facing away. Pull body upward until chin is above the bar, then lower down. Repeat for desired reps, maintaining controlled movement. View Example.
